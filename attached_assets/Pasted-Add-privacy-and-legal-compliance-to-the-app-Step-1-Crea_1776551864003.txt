Add privacy and legal compliance to the app.

Step 1 — Create a Privacy Policy page:
New file artifacts/travel-widget/src/pages/privacy.tsx
Route: /privacy — add to App.tsx, no auth required

Content written in plain English, not legalese.
Same T+L editorial styling as the rest of the app.
Playfair Display headings, Raleway body text.

Include these sections:

WHAT WE COLLECT
- Account information (name, email via Clerk)
- Travel party details you enter (names, ages, relationships)
- Travel preferences and style selections
- Favourite properties and hotel brands
- Loyalty program names and membership numbers (optional)
- Usage data — which features you use, when

WHY WE COLLECT IT
- To auto-fill travel booking forms on your behalf
- To match hotel and restaurant reviews to your preferences
- To generate your travel personality profile using AI
- To sync your profile to the browser extension

HOW WE USE AI
- Review scoring uses Anthropic's Claude API
- Your travel preferences are sent to Claude to score reviews
- Your profile data is used to generate a personality description
- We do not use your data to train AI models
- Anthropic's privacy policy applies to Claude API usage

CHILDREN'S DATA
- We collect ages of children in your travel party at your request
- This data is used solely for booking form auto-fill (guest ages)
- We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13
- Parents/guardians control all data entered about their children
- You can delete any traveler record at any time

YOUR RIGHTS (CCPA / GDPR)
- Access: request a copy of all your data
- Deletion: delete your account and all associated data
- Portability: export your data as JSON
- Opt-out: you can disable AI review scoring in preferences

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY
- Data stored in PostgreSQL database hosted by Replit/Neon
- Authentication managed by Clerk
- Loyalty numbers stored but never shared with third parties
- We do not sell your data. Ever.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES
- Clerk (authentication) — clerk.com/privacy
- Anthropic Claude API (AI review scoring) — anthropic.com/privacy
- Google Places API (hotel search) — policies.google.com/privacy
- Stripe (payments, when enabled) — stripe.com/privacy

CONTACT
- For privacy requests: [your email]
- To delete your account: use Settings > Delete Account in the app
- Response within 30 days as required by CCPA

UPDATES
- Last updated: [today's date]
- We'll notify users of material changes via email

Step 4 — Add data export and deletion:
New route GET /api/account/export
Returns all user data as a single JSON object:
{
  account: { userId, consentGivenAt },
  travelers: [...],
  preferences: {...},
  properties: [...],
  loyaltyPrograms: [...],
  tripProfiles: [...],
  reviewScores: [...],
  exportedAt: timestamp
}

New route DELETE /api/account
Deletes ALL records for userId across all tables:
travelers, preferences, properties, loyalty_programs,
trip_profiles, review_scores, subscriptions
Returns { deleted: true }
